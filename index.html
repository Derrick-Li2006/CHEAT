About the Game

CHEAT 是一款叙事驱动的心理恐怖游戏。 你将探索被扭曲的现实世界，面对自己内心的秘密与恐惧。 记忆、谎言与真相之间的界限，正在逐渐崩塌……

CHEAT is a narrative-driven psychological horror game.
You will explore a distorted reality, uncover hidden secrets, and confront your deepest fears.
The line between memory, lies, and truth is slowly collapsing...
